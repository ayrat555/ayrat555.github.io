Hey everyone! 👋

I'm excited to share that Timeless Solitaire Collection is now fully released on Steam! 🎉

If you've enjoyed my open source work over the years, this is also a great way to support me by checking out the game.

The full version includes seven games in the collection:

- 🃏 Magical Gathering (Klondike variant)
- ♣️ Club Calculus (Calculation variant)
- ⚔️ Age of Warriors (FreeCell variant)
- 🦇 Family Reunion (Osmosis variant)
- 🚜 Farm Commotion (Spider variant)
- 🎭 Bad Habits (Yukon variant)
- 🦁 Zoo (memory card game)

Each game has its own theme, mechanics, and variations, designed to offer relaxing gameplay with room for thoughtful strategy. The goal of the collection has always been simple: take timeless solitaire games and present them in a calm, modern way.

If you enjoy the game, leaving a review on Steam really helps more players discover it.

👉 [https://store.steampowered.com/app/4029350/Timeless_Solitaire_Collection](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4029350/Timeless_Solitaire_Collection)

Hope you enjoy the full collection!
